Procedures & Guidelines

DO submit a ticket about the CORE application if there is a malfunction with the way the application is operating. Please include what you were doing, the account and/or server number, and the full text of the error as it appears on your screen.

DO submit a ticket if there is functionality missing from the CORE application. Make sure your Champion approves and prioritizes the request.

DO send e-mail to rtshd@lists.rackspace.com for general information and questions.

DO report access problems and total application failures to your manager immediately.

After Hours Emergency Procedures

  1. Verify that other Rackers are having a problem with CORE. If the problem is with MyRackspace send an email to web911@lists.rackspace.com. If the problem is with any other system then email systems911@rackspace.com.
  2. Submit a ticket and mark as an "Emergency", if possible.
  3. Report the problem to your supervisor.
  4. Your supervisor will make a determination if this is a problem that can't be worked around. If not, your supervisor will call the Service Desk at x700-4357.
  5. That's it! Someone from the Service Desk will contact you to help diagnose the problem.
Thank you for following this procedure when reporting problems after normal business hours. With your help, issues will be solved quickly and efficiently so we can get you back up and running in as short a time as possible.
